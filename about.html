About

DipStories is the interdisciplinary practice of Dimitra Peppa, moving between photography, graphic design, landscape architecture, and research.

The work is guided by atmosphere, observation, and the emotional qualities of space. It is interested in how images, landscapes, and visual narratives can hold memory, care, and quiet intensity.
