About

DipStories is a multidisciplinary creative practice rooted in observation, atmosphere, and thoughtful making.

DipStories brings together photography, graphic design, landscape architecture, and research through a calm and reflective approach. The work is shaped by sensitivity to place, storytelling, and the emotional quality of visual and spatial experiences.

Across different formats, the aim is to create work that feels grounded, clear, and quietly memorable. Sometimes that means an image, sometimes a layout, sometimes a spatial idea, and sometimes a research-based exploration.

Rather than separating disciplines too rigidly, DipStories sees them as connected ways of noticing, interpreting, and communicating.
